Marly Creative Concepts LLC  ·  Last updated: March 28, 2026

Marly Creative Concepts LLC ("Marly," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website marly.cc and related subdomains, including review.marly.cc (collectively, the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding that information.

By using the Site, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you submit a project brief through our intake form at marly.cc/brief or contact us through the Site, you may provide:

  • Your name and email address
  • Company or brand name
  • Brand assets and creative direction details (logos, color codes, typography, URLs)
  • Campaign goals, copy, and project specifications

Form submissions are processed through Formspree, a third-party form backend. Formspree's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit the Site, we automatically collect certain information through Google Analytics (Measurement ID: G-NM1YHB24BE), including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website or source
  • Browser type, device type, and screen resolution
  • Approximate geographic location (city/region level, not precise)
  • Interactions with Site content

Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect this data. Google's data practices are described in their privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Review Portal

If you are a client accessing work-in-progress materials at review.marly.cc, that subdomain does not collect any personal data beyond what is logged by the hosting provider (Vercel) in standard server logs (IP address, browser type, access timestamps). No cookies are set on the review portal. Materials displayed on the review portal are not final deliverables and remain the property of Marly Creative Concepts until formal delivery per the terms of your service agreement.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and process project briefs
  • Deliver the services you have engaged us to perform
  • Understand how visitors use the Site so we can improve it
  • Communicate with you about your projects or our services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

The Site uses cookies set by Google Analytics to understand visitor behavior. These cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us analyze traffic patterns and improve the Site.

Types of cookies used:

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics): Track page views, session duration, and traffic sources. These cookies do not identify you personally.
  • Essential cookies: Limited to basic functionality required for the Site to operate (e.g., form submission state).

We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or social media tracking scripts.

Your cookie choices: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. You can also opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout. Blocking analytics cookies will not affect your ability to use the Site.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We share information only with the following service providers, strictly as needed to operate the Site and deliver our services:

  • Formspree — processes form submissions from marly.cc/brief and marly.cc/contact
  • Google Analytics — provides website usage analytics
  • Vercel — hosts the Site and review portal
  • GitHub — hosts our codebase (no client personal data is stored in repositories)

We may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Marly Creative Concepts, our clients, or the public.

Data Retention

  • Form submissions (active clients): Retained for the duration of the client relationship plus 12 months, then deleted.
  • Inquiries that do not result in an engagement: Deleted within 12 months of submission.
  • Analytics data: Retained in Google Analytics per Google's default retention settings (14 months), after which it is automatically aggregated and anonymized.
  • Server logs: Retained by Vercel per their standard log retention policies.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Opt out of analytics tracking (see Cookies section above)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below. We will respond within 30 days.

For California residents (CCPA): We do not sell personal information. You have the right to know what personal information we collect and to request its deletion. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

For EU/EEA visitors (GDPR): Our legal basis for processing your data is legitimate interest (improving our Site and services) and, where applicable, your consent (submitting a form constitutes consent to process the information you provide). You have the right to withdraw consent, access your data, request portability, or lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including using HTTPS encryption across all Site pages and subdomains, and limiting access to personal data to those who need it to provide our services. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed at individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.
