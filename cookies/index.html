This Cookie Policy explains how marly.cc and its subdomains (the "Site") use cookies and similar tracking technologies. This policy supplements our Privacy Policy.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help site operators understand how visitors interact with their pages and are widely used across the web.

Cookies We Use

Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics (Measurement ID: G-NM1YHB24BE) to understand how visitors use the Site. These cookies collect information such as pages visited, time on site, traffic source, browser type, and approximate geographic location.

Google Analytics cookies do not identify you personally. Data is aggregated and used solely to improve the Site.

Cookie Provider Purpose Duration
_ga Google Distinguishes unique visitors 2 years
_ga_* Google Maintains session state 2 years

For more information on how Google processes this data, see Google's privacy policy.

Essential Cookies

The Site may use limited essential cookies required for basic functionality, such as maintaining form submission state. These cookies are strictly necessary and cannot be disabled without affecting Site functionality.

Cookies We Do Not Use

We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, social media trackers, or any third-party marketing cookies.

Your Choices

You can manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or be notified about cookies. Refer to your browser's help documentation for instructions.
  • Google Analytics opt-out: Install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on to prevent Google Analytics from collecting data about your visits.

Blocking analytics cookies will not affect your ability to browse the Site or submit a project brief.
